SNL 11th Annual Halloween Costume Party

Join us for a spooky and fun-filled Halloween event!

Event Details

Date: October 31, 2024

Time: All day event

Costume Contest & Voting

There will be prizes for:

  • Best Individual Costume
  • Best Group Costume

How to participate: You can submit your costume photo by emailing LMORRIS@augusta.edu before 3:00 PM on the day of the event.

Voting: Online costume voting will be announced via email on 11/1 and will run through 11/4. Winners will be announced in the newsletter on 11/6.

Participation Rules

1) The contest is for GCC staff in CN, AN, HK, and DTN buildings only.

2) You can participate in only one category (Individual or Group).

Additional Fun!

Don't forget to get a treat from the SNL Candy Cart!

Stop by and meet SNL members when you see (or hear!) the treats cart. You can also locate the treat-filled goodie bags set up in your work area!
